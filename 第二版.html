声调 Tones

Rule 1 · 大哥到小弟 (who gets the mark)

a > o > e > i > u > ü

If there's an a, it takes the mark. No a? then o or e. The big brothers go first.

Rule 2 · iu & ui (mark the last one)

iū  ·  uǐ

When i and u are together, the mark goes on whichever comes last.

Rule 3 · little ü

nǚ  ·  lǜ

The mark sits on top of ü. And after j q x y, ü drops its two dots → u.
